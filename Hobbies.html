Hobbies

This is a list of my three main hobbies that I enjoy

Snowboarding

Snowboarding has been a huge part of my life since I moved to Vancouver. I began to snowboard competitively at 14 years old when I tried out for the BC Snowboard Development team.
After a successful tryout I began to take snowboarding more seriously as I travelled around BC participating in various competitions.
I picked up a few sponsors along the way who helped me raise funds for transportation and contest entry fees.

Travelling

Over the past few years I have had amazing opportunities to travel the world. Some notable places I have been to are Malaysia, Thailand and Mexico.

Fitness

Since my Junior year of high school, I have always believed that exercise and fitness can help improve mental health and promote healthy habits.
I have tried my best to stick with a consistent gym schedule for the past few years and to maintain a healthy diet. I am hoping that having easy access to a gym in University will help me stick with the schedule I created.
